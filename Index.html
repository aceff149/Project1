About Me

I am originally from Texas and made my way to Nashville in 2019. I have worked in the Customer Service Industry my whole career which lead me to learning how to make trucker hats. I have made hats for companies, group vacations, themed parties and so much more! I really started this company when my mom and her group of friends came to me to create Breast Cancer Battle Boxes. As these women are survivors of Breast Cancer and having my mother fight herself, there was no better calling than to help more women fighting Breast Cancer than with a Battle Box. My families cancer journey did not start there as my dad fought and died to Glioblastoma in 2017. Fighting cancer is no easy task and I want to help any of those in need. The Cancer Battle Box can be custom to any type of cancer! This is the main purpose of what has lead me to create fun and funny hats, stickers, etc. Message me for any fun ideas you are looking for in your next trip, party, or special event as I would love to be apart of it!
